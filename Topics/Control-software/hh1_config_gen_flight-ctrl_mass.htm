Adjusting ROV Center of Mass

See Also for links to information about setting flight control parameters.

The control system uses information about the ROV center of mass (CoM) to determine how much thrust to generate from each thruster to perform commanded movements. When you add tooling, a skid, or other heavy equipment to the ROV, the vehicle CoM can change substantially. This affects operations by changing the commanded flight line, as shown in the table below.

Use the CENTER OF MASS ADJUSTMENT panel (shown below) to change the control system CoM values to more closely match the vehicleâ€™s current true CoM.

1. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
2. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
3. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap FLIGHT CONTROL.
4. In the CENTER OF MASS ADJUSTMENT panel, tap the plus or minus buttons to change the CoM as desired.
5. After you make adjustments, fly the vehicle and note any flight deviation. Adjust the CoM as necessary until you have corrected the flight deviation.
