HPU Settings

For each hydraulic circuit in the ROV system, you can set the maximum circuit pressure and the pressure set point at startup.

1. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
2. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
3. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap HPU SETTINGS.
4. Tap the tab for the desired HPU circuit (for example, ROV AUX).

5. Enter the desired values for:
● Maximum circuit pressure (the highest pressure that can appear on HPU pressure gauges)
● Minimum circuit pressure set point (pressure goes to this value when you press the lower gray area of an HPU pressure gauge)
● Maximum circuit pressure set point (pressure goes to this value when you press the upper gray area of an HPU pressure gauge)
6. Tap SAVE.
