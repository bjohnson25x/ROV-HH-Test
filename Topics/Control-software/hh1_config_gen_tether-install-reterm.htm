Installing, Reterminating, or Shortening Tether

See Also for links to information about configuring TMS tether.

Installing a New Tether

1. Before you change values for TMS tether settings, use the FLIGHT screen TMS CONTROL panel to disable TMS operation. (This action disables power to the drum motor, pinch wheel motor, and latches.)
2. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
3. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
4. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap TMS.
5. Enter the tether length:
a. If you know the total tether length in meters or feet, enter the length in the panel. Be sure that you are entering the correct number for the units used (meters or feet).
b. If you do not know the tether length, fully install the tether on the drum, counting the layers as the tether is installed. Then count the wraps that make up any partial layer (see Counting Layers and Wraps), and enter the total number of layers and wraps.
6. You do not need to enter information in the TETHER LEFT ON DRUM box. When the ROV is docked to the TMS, the TETHER LEFT ON DRUM setting automatically changes to match TOTAL TETHER LENGTH.

Reterminating or Shortening Tether

1. Fully dock the ROV to the TMS.
2. Measure the length that was removed from the tether.
3. Use the FLIGHT screen TMS CONTROL panel to disable TMS operation.
4. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
5. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
6. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap TMS.
7. Note the number displayed in the TOTAL TETHER LENGTH box and subtract the length that was removed. Then enter the new length.

Changing the TOTAL TETHER LENGTH does not affect or change the MINIMUM TETHER ON DRUM entry. Also, the TETHER LEFT ON DRUM setting will automatically change to match TOTAL TETHER LENGTH when the ROV is docked to the TMS.
