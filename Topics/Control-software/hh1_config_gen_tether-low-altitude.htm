Adjusting TMS Low-Altitude Threshold

See Also for links to information about configuring TMS tether.

If the TMS moves too close to the seafloor, the TMS altitude indicator on the FLIGHT screen turns orange.

To adjust the threshold for the TMS low-altitude indication:

1. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
2. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
3. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap TMS.
4. In the TMS LOW ALTITUDE THRESHOLD, tap the UP or DOWN button to increase or decrease the altitude.
