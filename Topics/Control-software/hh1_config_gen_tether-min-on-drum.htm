Changing the Minimum Tether to be Left on Drum

See Also for links to information about configuring TMS tether.

This setting defines the length of tether that must remain on the drum to prevent tether from being completely run off the drum.

1. Use the FLIGHT screen TMS CONTROL panel to disable TMS operation.
2. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
3. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
4. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap TMS.
5. Enter the desired number in MINIMUM TETHER ON DRUM. The factory-set safety default is 50 meters.

Caution!  Paying out all the tether on the drum can damage the tether and components to which it is connected. If you enter a lower MINIMUM TETHER ON DRUM value than the factory-set default, use caution during tether operations.

Changing the MINIMUM TETHER ON DRUM setting does not affect or change the TOTAL TETHER LENGTH and TETHER LEFT ON DRUM settings.
