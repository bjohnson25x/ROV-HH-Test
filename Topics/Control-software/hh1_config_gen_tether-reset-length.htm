Resetting Tether Length

See Also for links to information about configuring TMS tether.

Tether settings can be lost if tether was manually spooled from the drum while the system was shut down.

To reset tether length, use one of the options below.

Option 1: ROV can be docked to TMS

If possible, dock the ROV to the TMS. Docking causes the control system to reset the TOTAL TETHER LENGTH display to the existing entered length. No other action is necessary unless the tether was shortened while the system was shut down.

Option 2: ROV is separated from the TMS

1. Use the FLIGHT screen TMS CONTROL panel to disable TMS operation.
2. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
3. On any CONFIGURATION screen, tap the GENERAL tab.
4. On the list on the left side of the screen, tap TMS.
5. Count the layers and partial layers (wraps) of tether on the drum as accurately as possible.
6. Enter the layer measurement.
7. If ROV operations continue, enable the TMS and look at the drum to confirm that enough tether remains on the drum to avoid slippage and damage. If you can reasonably do so, increase the MINIMUM TETHER ON DRUM by 10-20 meters to avoid overextending the tether.
8. As soon as possible, dock the ROV to the TMS. This resets the tether counter to zero, and makes equal the TOTAL TETHER LENGTH and TETHER LEFT ON DRUM.
