Rate Hand Controller (RHC)

See Also for links to information about using specific tactile controls.

You can use the rate hand controller (RHC) to operate a rate-controlled slave arm (for example, a 5-function RigMaster slave arm or a 7-function CONAN, ORION, or ATLAS slave arm). You can also use the RHC to operate other rate-controlled tools.

To operate rate-controlled slave arms, you can also use:

● Screen controls (see Hydraulics for Rate-Controlled Manipulators).
● Switches on the tool control pod (see Assigning Valve Operation to the Tool Pod).
Rate Hand Controller Functions
Item Description To Activate To Deactivate
Jaw open Pair of jaw-open momentary push-button switches on left and right sides, allowing left- or right-hand operation with thumb. Press jaw-open Release jaw-open
Jaw close Pair of jaw-close momentary push-buttons on left and right sides, allowing left- or right-hand operation with thumb. Press jaw-close Release jaw-close
Jaw lock Locks the jaw closed. Press and hold jaw-close for 5 seconds Press jaw-open
Wrist rotate Twist hand grip around its vertical axis in either direction from the neutral position. Twist and hold Release
Azimuth Rock the grip to either side from the neutral position. Rock and hold Release
