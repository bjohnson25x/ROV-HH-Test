About Touch Screen Features

See Also for links to information about touch screen controls.

You can perform most ROV system operations using a touch screen on the control console.

Touch Types
Touch Type Action Result
Tap Very briefly touch your finger on a screen control (like a button). Performs the designated function for that screen control.
Long press Touch and hold your finger on a screen control (like a button) until a white circle displays. Displays a context menu with additional choices.
Drag Touch the screen control (for example, a gauge slider or scroll bar) and drag your finger across the screen. The gauge indicator moves or options scroll on the screen.
