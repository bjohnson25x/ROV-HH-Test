Navigating to and Controlling Screen Elements
Symbol Type Function Example
Panel or menu display/hide arrow

Tapping the blue arrow displays a panel containing additional options or data or a drop-down menu.

Tapping the white arrow hides the panel or drop-down menu.

Link arrow Tapping the link arrow takes you away from the current task to a different screen display or task.

Drag lines Touching and dragging moves a sliding control.

Box close Tapping the “X” closes the box.

Double arrows

Tapping the blue double arrow displays additional buttons or items in a category.

Tapping the white double arrow removes the additional buttons or items.

Drop-down arrow

Tapping the arrows displays a drop-down box of options.

Tapping a second time removes the drop-down box.

Panel expand/ reduce arrow

Tapping the arrows in a standard panel expands the panel’s data and options.

Tapping the arrows in an expanded panel reduces the data and options back to standard-panel size.

Scroll control A grayed item at the top or bottom of a list indicates that more items are available. Move your finger up or down to scroll through the list; a scroll bar will also appear on the right side of the list.
