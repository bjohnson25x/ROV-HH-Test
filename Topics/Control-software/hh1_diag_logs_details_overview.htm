About Diagnostics Logs for Events and Active Issues

See Also for links to information about logs for events and active issues.

Purpose: Lists events and active issues to aid in troubleshooting and diagnosing problems.

Access: In the DIAGNOSTICS section of the main drop-down menu, tap LOGS.

During operation, the control system notes significant happenings and lets you view lists of those happenings. The happenings are classified as issues and events.
