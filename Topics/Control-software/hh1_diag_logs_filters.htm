Applying Filters to Issue/Event Logs

See Also for links to information about logs for events and active issues.

In the ACTIVE ISSUES and EVENT HISTORY lists, filters let you display only the items that you are interested in viewing.

In this example, two filters are being applied, one for the type of event and one for the ROV subsystems. With both filters applied, the event log will show all alarms and warnings (but no information events) for the console and topside, and for the power and compensation subsystems.
