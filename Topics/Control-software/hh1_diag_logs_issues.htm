Viewing Logs of Active Issues

See Also for links to information about logs for events and active issues.

The ACTIVE ISSUES list shows active issues (issues that are not yet resolved and issues that are resolved but have not been acknowledged).

Issues are color-coded based on their importance to ROV system operation:

● Yellow - Information (a relevant occurrence has taken place)
● Orange - Warning (a situation likely needs attention)
● Red - Alarm (a situation needs urgent attention)
