8-Liter Compensator/Reservoir Diagnostics

See Also for links to diagnostics information for specific compensators and reservoirs.

The control system connects to all ROV-installed 8-liter compensators and 8-liter reservoirs through a position sensor array (PSA). Diagnostics information for 8-liter compensators and reservoirs consists primarily of PSA diagnostics information.

Note:  To view fluid levels for all compensators and reservoirs or to view or set high-volume and low-volume alarm levels, use the FLIGHT screen COMPS panel.

You can display PSA diagnostics information in multiple ways.

See how

From the Main Menu:

1. Tap DIAGNOSTICS.
2. Tap SYSTEM.
3. When the diagnostics dashboard displays, tap the button for the desired subsystem (like HYDRAULICS, COMPENSATION, or LIGHTS) on the desired platform (like ROV or TMS).

Or from any DIAGNOSTICS screen:

1. Tap the platform drop-down button on the upper left, and then tap the desired platform (like ROV or TMS).
2. Tap a button on the left to select the desired subsystem (like HYDRAULICS, COMPENSATION, or LIGHTS).

Because 8-liter compensators and reservoirs communicate with the control system through the ROV PSA, select the ROV platform, the COMPENSATION subsystem, and the PSA device.

Information in the COMPONENT DETAILS panel apply to PSA diagnostics rather than to compensator and reservoir status.

Compensation Calibration

Because 8-liter compensators and reservoirs use a PSA to communicate with the control system, they can be automatically calibrated by the control system. No additional calibration is needed.
