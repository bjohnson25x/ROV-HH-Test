To view status and diagnostics information for the video subsystem, navigate to the subsystem screen display.

See how

From the Main Menu:

1. Tap DIAGNOSTICS.
2. Tap SYSTEM.
3. When the diagnostics dashboard displays, tap the button for the desired subsystem (like HYDRAULICS, COMPENSATION, or LIGHTS) on the desired platform (like ROV or TMS).

Or from any DIAGNOSTICS screen:

1. Tap the platform drop-down button on the upper left, and then tap the desired platform (like ROV or TMS).
2. Tap a button on the left to select the desired subsystem (like HYDRAULICS, COMPENSATION, or LIGHTS).

Tap VIDEO to display summary information for all cameras and other components of the video subsystem.

Video Subsystem Diagnostics for TAVS

The TAVS video subsystem diagnostics screen displays video routing details for all video outputs from the ROV, TMS, and topside.

Video Subsystem Diagnostics for HDEVS

The HDEVS video subsystem diagnostics screen displays status information for equipment like video decoders and the video computer. The same video subsystem screen is displayed for all ROV system platforms.
