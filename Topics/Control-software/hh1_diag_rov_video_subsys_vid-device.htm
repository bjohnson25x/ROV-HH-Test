Video Device Diagnostics Details

See Also for links to diagnostics information for specific video devices and systems.

A platform’s video diagnostics subsystem can include:

● Cameras
● Pan and tilt actuators
● Video decoders (HDEVS only)

Cameras

In the diagnostics screen for a camera, the COMPONENT DETAILS section contains status information specific to that camera type. Information can include:

● Firmware versions
● Video format
● Zoom, focus, and shutter status
● Color and white balance status
