Purpose: The TMS diagnostics screens let you view status data for TMS equipment.

Access: On the DIAGNOSTICS dashboard, tap the button for the desired TMS subsystem.

Caution!  When you are testing the docked TMS and ROV on deck, pay out the smallest amount of tether possible to test the functionality. Paying out too much length can damage the tether.

Diagnostics information is available for the following TMS subsystems:

● Compensation (similar to ROV; see About the Compensation Subsystem Diagnostics Display)
● Flight
● Hydraulics (for a UHD-II hydraulic TMS only)
● Lights (similar to ROV; see About the Lights Subsystem Diagnostics Display)
● Power
● Tether control
● Video (similar to ROV; see Video Subsystem Diagnostics Display)

Note:  The number and type of component-level screens vary depending on the number and type of installed devices.
