You should calibrate the TMS latches:

● As part of system mobilization before the ROV is docked to the TMS
● After latches have been serviced or replaced
● If latch icons on the screen are orange

You can access the latch CALIBRATE button from:

● The TMS panel of the DIAGNOSTICS screen
● The TMS panel of the FLIGHT screen, by long pressing anywhere in the panel to display the context menu

To calibrate latches:

1. Ensure that the ROV is not docked to the TMS.
2. Enable TMS control.
3. Tap CALIBRATION. The latches run through a calibration sequence without further input from you.
