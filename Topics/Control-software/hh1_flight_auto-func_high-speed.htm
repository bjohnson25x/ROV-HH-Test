HIGH SPEED Features
Purpose Lets the ROV operate at the maximum allowed speed in response to joystick movement.
Access On the FLIGHT screen, tap OTHER and then tap HIGH SPEED.
Turning on/off

Tap HIGH SPEED.

The ROV has a high-speed mode and low-speed mode:

● In high speed mode, moving the flight joystick to its maximum deflection provides the highest allowed thrust for most ROV movements. (Yaw movements remain at the low speed setting.) You can change the value for highest allowed thrust to be less than 100%, as described in Setting Maximum Thruster Power and Displacement Speed.
● In low speed mode, you can set joystick sensitivity (called low-speed gains) so that maximum deflection provides less than the highest amount of allowed thrust. By increasing joystick sensitivity in this way, you can move the ROV more precisely.

If the HIGH SPEED button is off, the ROV speed setting is low. The speed setting is high only if the HIGH SPEED button is on.

The low speed mode is the default setting.

Limits for low-speed joystick sensitivity are part of each user profile. For information on setting sensitivity, see Scaling Low-Speed Thrust on the Flight Joystick.
