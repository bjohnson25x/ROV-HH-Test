REVERSE Features
Purpose Reverses thruster response to flight joystick movement and CRUISE set points for fore/aft and starboard/port movement. Used when flying the ROV backward.
Access On the FLIGHT screen, tap OTHER and then tap REVERSE.
Turning on/off

Tap REVERSE.
Function interactions

Though CRUISE control functions in REVERSE mode, you cannot switch between CRUISE and REVERSE when CRUISE is turned on.

All other auto functions work in REVERSE mode, but their orientation remains normal. For example, the AUTO HEADING set point and TRIMS set points are always relative to the front of the ROV.

If you are flying the ROV backward (for example, flying back to the TMS while looking at bullet camera output), changing to REVERSE mode makes flight more intuitive by reversing thruster response to flight joystick movement and CRUISE set points for fore/aft and starboard/port movement. Vertical and yaw movements are not affected.

For example, in REVERSE mode, pushing the joystick forward flies the ROV straight aft relative to its normal orientation, and moving the joystick to the left moves the ROV to starboard. Similarly, tapping FORWARD in the CRUISE panel adjusts the thrust set point to aft.

The FLIGHT compass display indicates whether the ROV is operating in REVERSE mode.
