About AutoFollow Manipulator Tracking

See Also for links to AutoFollow (manipulator tracking) information.

Purpose: Lets a pan-and-tilt-mounted camera automatically follow a moving manipulator arm.

Access: Tap the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS button, and then select the camera to be used with AutoFollow.

AutoFollow uses a manipulator's position feedback to automatically adjust a pan and tilt to keep the manipulator's jaws in sight of the camera mounted on that pan and tilt. This means that moving the manipulator arm automatically moves the pan and tilt so that the camera follows the slave arm jaws.

AutoFollow can be used only on manipulators that are controlled with position feedback, like the TITAN 4 and CONAN 7P.

Only pan and tilt units installed on the ROV can be used with AutoFollow. Pan and tilts on other platforms (like the TMS or a skid) cannot use AutoFollow.

WARNING!  Before you test or use AutoFollow, ensure that people and equipment cannot be struck by the moving manipulator arm.
