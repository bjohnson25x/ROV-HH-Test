Identifying Pan and Tilt Mounting Location for AutoFollow

See Also for links to AutoTrack (survey operations) information.

For AutoFollow to work correctly, the control system must know the location of a pan and tilt relative to the manipulator that the pan and tilt is following. A factory-installed pan and tilt will be either:

● Mounted near the frame bottom; this is the most common position.
● Mounted on the upper light/sensor rack (also called the survey rack).

The illustration below shows the typical pan and tilt mounting position on the lower frame.

If a pan and tilt is mounted at either of these factory-installed positions, the control system automatically knows the location of the pan and tilt relative to the manipulator used for AutoFollow. If a pan and tilt has been moved from its factory-installed position, you must configure the pan and tilt so that AutoFollow knows the new location (see Pan and Tilt Configuration Properties).
