Camera Control with Screen, Tool Pod, and Joystick

See Also for links to information about camera operation and configuration.

Due to the limited focus and zoom speed of many cameras and the slight video latency (delay) in long-line systems, “press and hold” adjustments to the zoom and focus controls often overshoot, especially when the subject is close or the lens is zooming in the telephoto range. If possible, make small adjustments by quickly touching the control or quickly tapping the screen button, then pause briefly to view the effects of the change before you make further adjustments.

For information on assigning a pan and tilt to a camera, see Camera Configuration Properties.
