Camera Control Overview

See Also for links to information about camera operation and configuration.

Purpose: Selects and controls cameras on the ROV and TMS.

Access: On the FLIGHT screen, tap CAMERAS.

You can select and control cameras with any of these items:

● Buttons on the touch screen CAMERAS panel.
● Tool control pod.
● Flight joystick (camera zoom and focus only).

Cameras are typically mounted on pan and tilts to allow viewing angle adjustment.

For information on assigning a pan and tilt to a camera, see Camera Configuration Properties.
