About Compensation/Reservoir Monitoring

See Also for links to information about compensator/reservoir monitoring.

Purpose: For ROV and TMS compensators and reservoirs, the COMPENSATOR & RESERVOIR LEVELS panel lets you:

● View current and historic volume levels.
● Adjust low-level and high-level alarms.

Access: On the FLIGHT screen, tap COMP.

Long press the icon for a compensator/reservoir to access buttons for:

● Calibrating a compensator (for compensators that require calibration)
● Turning compensator/reservoir port power on and off
● Editing the compensator/reservoir label

Compensator Calibration

The 2.2-liter compensator and 20-liter reservoir require calibration. The 8-liter compensator/reservoir does not.
