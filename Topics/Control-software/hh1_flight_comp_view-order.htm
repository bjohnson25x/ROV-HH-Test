Changing Compensator or Reservoir Display Order

See Also for links to information about compensator/reservoir monitoring.

Note:  This feature is not available for all ROV systems.

You can change the order in which compensators and reservoirs appear in the COMPENSATOR AND RESERVOIR LEVELS panel.

1. From the FLIGHT screen, tap COMPS.
2. In the COMPENSATOR AND RESERVOIR LEVELS panel, long press a compensator (to change compensator display order) or a reservoir (to change reservoir display order) for the desired platform (ROV or TMS).
3. When the long press submenu displays, tap UPDATE SORTING.
4. When the list of compensators or reservoirs displays, drag the items in the list until they are in the desired order. The item at the top of the list displays first in the COMPENSATOR AND RESERVOIR LEVELS panel.
5. Tap SAVE.
