About Device Power Control (Device Power Panel)

See Also for links to information about controlling power to individual devices.

Purpose: Lets you view and control power status for individual devices in the ROV system.

Access: On the FLIGHT screen, tap DEVICE POWER.

For devices on the ROV system, you can:

● Turn power on/off an individual device
● Turn power on/off for all devices on a platform (ROV, TMS, or topside)
● View device power status and communication status
● Change a device's auto-on setting

Caution!  You should not typically set to Auto-On any device (like a light) that could overheat on deck.
