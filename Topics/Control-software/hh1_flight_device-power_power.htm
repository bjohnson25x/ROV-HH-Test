Viewing/Changing Device Power On/Off from the DEVICE POWER Panel

See Also for links to information about controlling power to individual devices.

The DEVICE POWER panel lets you switch power on or off to individual devices on the ROV system. (This is done by turning power to the node port on or off.)

Tap the drop-down menu button at the top left to choose the platform (topside, ROV, TMS, etc.) for devices to display. You can display devices for only one platform at a time.

You can group devices by type (like cameras, lights, and sensors) or by the nodes that the devices are attached to. (Keep in mind that you will only see devices and nodes that are on the selected platform.) Overall status (power and communications) of each device is displayed using a status indicator next to the device label. You can also navigate to a device's diagnostics page directly by long pressing the device and selecting the DIAGNOSTICS link.

To turn all devices for a platform on or off:

1. Display the DEVICE POWER panel.
2. Select the desired platform
3. Tap ALL.
4. Tap OFF or ON. OFF turns off all node ports attached to devices on that platform. ON turns on node ports that are set for auto-on. Ports not set for auto-on remain off.
