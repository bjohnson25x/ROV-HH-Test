Priming an Auxiliary Hydraulic Circuit (UHD-III Only)

See Also for links to information about operating and controlling hydraulic circuits.

You must prime the pump for an auxiliary (ISOL-8 pump) circuit if air has been introduced into that circuit. The priming function involves only the cylinders in the circuit being primed.

Priming a circuit’s pump can take several minutes, and you cannot prime more than one circuit at a time.

For detailed information on filling, bleeding, and priming an auxiliary hydraulic circuit on a UHD-III ROV system, see the appropriate service instruction.

You can access the PRIME button for a UHD-III auxiliary circuit from:

● The expanded view of the HYDRAULIC PRESSURE CONTROL panel (available on the FLIGHT screen or on any TOOLING screen).
● The DIAGNOSTICS screen for the ISOL-8 pump.

You cannot use the priming function for an auxiliary hydraulic circuit if circuit input pressure is greater than 211 bars (3000 psi).

Accessing the Priming Function from the FLIGHT Screen

1. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
2. Tap the HPU button.
3. When the HYDRAULIC PRESSURE CONTROL panel appears, tap the tab for the auxiliary circuit that you want to prime.
4. Ensure that circuit input pressure is no greater than 211 bars (3000 psi).
5. Expand the panel.
6. Tap PRIME, and slide the lock symbol to the right.

The percent complete for the priming process appears below the PRIME button.

Accessing the Priming Function from the ISOL-8 Pump DIAGNOSTICS Screen

To access the PRIME button from the ISOL-8 pump DIAGNOSTICS screen:

1. Ensure that the user profile for your control station includes supervisor permissions.
2. Ensure that circuit input pressure is no greater than 211 bars (3000 psi).
3. Display the ISOL-8 pump diagnostics page. (For instructions, see Diagnostics Information for a Single Device.)
4. Tap the OPTIONS button for the desired circuit.

5. In the OPTIONS panel, tap PRIME and slide the lock symbol to the right.
