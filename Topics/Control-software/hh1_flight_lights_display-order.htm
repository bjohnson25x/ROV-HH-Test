Changing Light Display Order in the LIGHTS Panel

See Also for links to information about ROV and TMS lights.

You can change the order in which lights appear in the LIGHTS panel.

1. From the FLIGHT screen, tap the LIGHTS button.
2. In the LIGHTS panel, long press the tab for the desired platform (ROV or TMS).
3. When the long press submenu displays, tap UPDATE SORTING.
4. When the list of lights displays for the platform displays, drag the lights in the list until they are in the desired order. The light at the top of the list displays first in the LIGHTS panel.
5. Tap SAVE.
