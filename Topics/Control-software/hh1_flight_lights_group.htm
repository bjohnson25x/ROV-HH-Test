Controlling Lights in Groups

See Also for links to information about ROV and TMS lights.

See Also for links to information about configuring and using light groups.

You can create a light group (containing two or more lights) and then control those lights together by:

● Turning the grouped lights on or off
● Changing intensity of all lights in the group

More than one light group can be active at a time.

Note:  When you assign a light to a group, you are not specifying an initial intensity for that light. A group is simply a designated set of lights that you want to control all together. To designate initial light intensity, you must use a light preset.

Once you have turned on lights in a group, you can turn off an individual light in that group by tapping the light's button. Remaining lights in the group stay on.

Controlling Light Group Intensity

If a group contains both dimmable and non-dimmable lights, intensity controls work only for the dimmable lights.
