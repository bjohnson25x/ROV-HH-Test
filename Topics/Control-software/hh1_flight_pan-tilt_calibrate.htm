Calibrating a Pan and Tilt

See Also for links to information about pan and tilt control and configuration.

WARNING!  Because the pan and tilt performs a series of automatic movements during calibration, keep personnel and equipment clear of the pan and tilt.

Calibrate a pan and tilt if:

● The pan and tilt platform was moved manually while power was down
● The entire device was repositioned on the ROV or TMS.
● The hard stops were moved.

Calibration is typically performed on deck but can be done at any time. Calibration should be performed during predive checks.

If the pan and tilt has not been calibrated, the message "UNCALIBRATED" displays under the pan and tilt control in the FLIGHT page CAMERAS panel.

To calibrate:

1. In the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel, long press the button for the camera that is mounted on the pan and tilt.
2. Tap CALIBRATE.
