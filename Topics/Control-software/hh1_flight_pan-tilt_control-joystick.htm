As shown below, the flight joystick contains a mini joystick button that lets you move a pan and tilt. A three-position selector switch on the joystick lets you indicate which pan and tilt will be moved by the mini joystick.

Use the TACTILE CONTROLS tab on any CONFIGURATION screen (Assigning Pan and Tilt Controls on the Flight Joystick) to indicate which pan and tilts are selected by the three-position thumb switch.

Dragging the mini joystick button down causes the pan and tilt to tilt up, and dragging the mini joystick up causes the pan and tilt to tilt down.

To move a pan and tilt with a flight joystick button:

1. Move the pan and tilt selector thumb switch to the desired position. (On the status bar, JSTICK P/T shows which pan and tilt is currently selected by the thumb switch.)
2. Press the flight joystick pan/tilt movement switch in the desired directions to move that pan and tilt. When you release the switch, the pan and tilt stops moving.

The same pan and tilt can be assigned to, and controlled by, both the right and left flight joysticks and the tool control pod joysticks. However, while a pan and tilt is executing a command from one joystick, the pan and tilt disregards commands from all other joysticks.
