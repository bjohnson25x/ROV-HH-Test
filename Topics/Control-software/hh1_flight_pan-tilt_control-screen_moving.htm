Moving a Pan and Tilt Using Screen Controls

See Also for links to information about pan and tilt control and configuration.

To move a pan and tilt using the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel, first select the camera assigned to that pan and tilt. Then drag the pointer (arrows) in the pan and tilt panel in the desired direction. When you remove your finger, the pan and tilt stops moving and the pointer returns to the center of the control.

Note:  Dragging the pointer down causes the pan and tilt to tilt up. Dragging the pointer up causes the pan and tilt to tilt down.

The pointer's color indicates the pan and tilt state:

● Gray pointer = selected camera is not assigned to a pan and tilt, or pan and tilt power is off.
● Blue pointer = selected camera's pan and tilt is powered and available.
