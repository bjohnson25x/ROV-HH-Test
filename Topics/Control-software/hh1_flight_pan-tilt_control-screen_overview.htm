Touch Screen Pan and Tilt Control: Overview

See Also for links to information about pan and tilt control and configuration.

The FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel provides:

● Touch screen control for selecting, enabling, and moving a pan and tilt.
● PRESETS/FOLLOW button for selecting a pan and tilt preset position or enabling manipulator AutoFollow.

For information on assigning a pan and tilt to a camera, see Camera Configuration Properties.
