Using Pan and Tilt Preset Positions

See Also for links to information about pan and tilt control and configuration.

Moving a Pan and Tilt to a Preset Position

1. In the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel, select the camera that you wish to move.
2. Tap PRESETS/FOLLOW to display a list of preset positions for that camera's pan and tilt.
3. Tap the button for the desired preset.

Creating a New Preset

1. In the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel, select the desired camera.
2. Move the camera's pan and tilt to the desired position for the preset.
3. Tap PRESETS/FOLLOW.
4. Tap +ADD PRESET.
5. On the keyboard, enter a name for the preset.

Deleting or Renaming a Preset

1. In the FLIGHT screen CAMERAS panel, select the desired camera.
2. Tap PRESETS/FOLLOW.
3. Long press the button for the preset that you are deleting or renaming.
4. Tap DELETE or RENAME.
