Selecting a Pan and Tilt

Press the buttons shown above to scroll up and down through the list of all ROV and TMS pan and tilts. Stop scrolling to select a pan and tilt. On the status bar, CSOLE P/T shows which pan and tilt is currently being controlled by the tool control pod joystick.

The same pan and tilt can be assigned to, and controlled by, both the right and left flight joysticks and the tool control pod joysticks. However, while a pan and tilt is executing a command from one joystick, the pan and tilt disregards commands from all other joysticks.

Moving a Pan and Tilt with the Tool Control Pod Joystick

1. Select the pan and tilt with the buttons on the tool control pod. Check the status bar CSOLE P/T to ensure that you have selected the desired joystick.
2. Move the pan and tilt joystick up, down, left, or right.

These pan and tilt movements are proportional; the father you move the joystick, the faster the pan and tilt moves.

Moving the joystick down causes the pan and tilt to tilt up. Moving the joystick up causes the pan and tilt to tilt down.
