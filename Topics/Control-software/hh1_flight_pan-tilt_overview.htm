About Pan and Tilt Control

See Also for links to information about pan and tilt control and configuration.

Purpose: Lets you select and move pan and tilts.

Access: On the FLIGHT screen, tap CAMERAS.

You can select and control pan and tilts with any of these items:

● Buttons on the touch screen CAMERAS panel.
● Tool control pod joystick.
● Buttons on the flight joystick.

Pan and tilts typically support cameras, but they can also support instruments like sonars.

The same pan and tilt can be assigned to, and controlled by, both the right and left flight joysticks and the tool control pod joysticks. However, while a pan and tilt is executing a command from one joystick, the pan and tilt disregards commands from all other joysticks.

For information on assigning a pan and tilt to a camera, see Camera Configuration Properties.

WARNING!  When the ROV and TMS are on deck, disable all pan and tilts to prevent injury or equipment damage from accidental motion.
