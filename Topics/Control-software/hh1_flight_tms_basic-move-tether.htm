Basic Steps for Moving Tether

See Also for links to information about operating and controlling the TMS.

1. In the Status Bar, assign tether control to the left or right control station.
2. In the FLIGHT screen TMS CONTROL panel:
a. Set the desired tether speed limit (SLOW or FAST for HD and UHD-III; SLOW, MEDIUM, or FAST for UHD-II).
b. Tap TMS CONTROL to enable TMS motors.
3. To move the tether, use the foot pedal (for proportional control) or flight joystick trigger (for nonproportional control).
4. In the TMS CONTROL panel, use the tether direction and speed indicator to observe tether movement.
