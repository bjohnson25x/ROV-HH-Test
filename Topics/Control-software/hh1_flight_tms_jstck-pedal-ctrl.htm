Flight Joystick and Foot Pedal Tether Control
Device Function
Flight joystick
● Trigger-type momentary rocker switch moves tether with nonproportional control (moves tether at a set speed).
● Push button switch toggles between tether speed limits (SLOW or FAST on HD/UHD-III and SLOW or MEDIUM on UHD-II). Sets tether movement speed for both joystick trigger and foot pedal.
Foot pedal Provides proportional tether movement: the farther that you press the pedal toward the floor, the faster the tether moves. Movement is limited to the range for the selected speed (SLOW or FAST on HD/UHD-III; SLOW, MEDIUM, or FAST on UHD-II).
