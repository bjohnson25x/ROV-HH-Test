"Render" Versus "Pay Out"

To understand the TMS screen display, you must understand the difference between "render" and "pay out":

● Pay out = TMS motors are causing the spooling of tether off the drum.
● Render = Tether is being pulled off the drum by some force other than the TMS motors (for example, by ROV movement or water currents).

Rendering can occur when TMS motors are stopped, when they are paying in tether (if the force pulling on the tether is greater than the motor force), and when they are paying out (if the force is pulling tether out faster than the motors are being commanded to pay out).

TMS Heading

If the TMS contains a compass, you can view TMS heading information by going to the TMS panel of the DIAGNOSTICS dashboard and tapping FLIGHT (on the left side of the panel).
