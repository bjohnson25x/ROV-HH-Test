Descent: Launch Procedures

See Also for links to information about launching and recovering the ROV system.

Flight Control

Once the ROV has been lowered to its working depth and you are ready to operate it, turn on ROV HPU power and raise HPU pressure to the working level. Then enable ROV flight control.

If the ROV system uses a hydraulic TMS, also turn on the TMS HPU and raise pressure to its working level.

Lights

Turn on and adjust lights when you are ready to operate the ROV. You can adjust lights using light presets, and you can simultaneously turn on all lights in a light group. For more information on light groups and presets, see Controlling Lights in Groups and Controlling Lights with Presets.

Go to Flight Page

When you enter "Mark as Done" for the final item on the launch checklist, the FLIGHT screen automatically displays. Also, at any time during the launch process, you can tap GO TO FLIGHT PAGE to exit from the launch sequence.
