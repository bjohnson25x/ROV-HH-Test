Using the Launch and Recovery Guide

See Also for links to information about launching and recovering the ROV system.

Purpose: Walks you through typical predive and postdive checks and operations.

Access: On the Main Menu, tap LAUNCH AND RECOVERY.

Caution!   Be sure to comply with your company's required procedures for launch and recovery operations.

The launch and recovery guide provides a general list of recommended checks and operations. You are not required to use the guide to launch and recover an ROV system, and the guide is not intended as a complete list of all checks that can be performed.

The appearance of screens in the launch and recovery guide varies with ROV system equipment configuration. (For example, if the ROV system uses the UHD-III hydraulic configuration, you will not see options that are specific to the HD/UHD-II hydraulic configuration.) Be sure to comply with your company’s procedures for launch and recovery operations, and use instructions in this manual only as a guide.

Screen Guides for Launch and Recovery

When you are finished with each screen of the launch or recovery sequence, tap MARK AS DONE in the top right. This places a check mark next to the link for that screen in the navigation bar on the left. When you have marked all screens for that section as done, a check mark displays next to the link for that section in the navigation bar near the top.

Launching the ROV and TMS

1. From the Main Menu, tap LAUNCH AND RECOVERY and then tap LAUNCH. The DIVE LOG screen then displays, and the launch wizard walks you through the major areas of a launch:
— Dive log
— Power
— Hydraulics
— Descent
2. Enter the customer name, dive ID, and dive description.

3. Tap MARK AS DONE.
4. Continue through the wizard until you have completed each launch section.

Recovering the ROV and TMS

1. From the Main Menu, tap LAUNCH AND RECOVERY and then tap RECOVERY. The Flight Control screen displays, and the recovery wizard walks you through the major steps of recovering the ROV and TMS:
— Ascent
— Surface
— Dive review
2. Assign joystick control and enable flight control.

3. Tap MARK AS DONE.
4. Continue through the wizard until you have completed each recovery section.
