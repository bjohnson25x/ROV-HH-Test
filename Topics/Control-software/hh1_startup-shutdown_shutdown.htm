Emergency Shutdown

For emergency ROV system shutdown, press one of the emergency-stop (E-stop) buttons. This action interrupts power to the ROV and TMS.

Large, red emergency-stop buttons are mounted on the control console and the PDU.

To reset, twist the emergency-stop button clockwise until it releases.

Standard Shutdown

1. From the FLIGHT screen POWER panel, turn off power to the TMS and ROV:
a. Turn off ROV HPU power.
b. Turn off ROV instrument power.
c. For the UHD-II only, turn off TMS HPU power.
d. Turn off TMS instrument power.
2. Shut down the console:
a. From the Status Bar, tap the PROFILE drop-down on the far right.
b. Tap SHUTDOWN.
c. When the SHUTDOWN button displays, drag the arrow to unlock icon. The control system software ends all software processes and shuts down the control system and the client computers.
3. Turn off the power strip on the 19-inch rack to turn off the RTC.
4. If the ROV system has a video computer, see About the HDEVS Video Computer App for information on shutting down the video computer.
5. If it is appropriate to turn off the PDU, turn off all PDU breakers and then turn off control power.
