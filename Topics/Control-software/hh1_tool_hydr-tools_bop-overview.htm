Purpose: Lets you perform a BOP fluid intervention using a hot stab tool and fluid pumped from the ISOL-8 pump.
Access: From the Main Menu, tap TOOLING, and then tap the TOOLS tab near the top right.

Note:  The BOP intervention function is available only for ROV systems that have a license for an 8-cylinder ISOL-8 pump.

Caution!  Be aware of all information and follow all steps listed or referred to in section and all other BOP sections. Failure to consider all steps and to execute them correctly can cause the BOP intervention to fail.

Caution!  Follow all applicable steps and observe all safety precautions in your company’s BOP intervention procedures. Before you begin an intervention, ensure that no instructions in your company’s BOP intervention procedures conflict with procedures listed in the ROV technical manual.

To perform a BOP intervention:

● The ROV system must be equipped with an 8-cylinder ISOL-8 pump, and the license for that pump must be installed.
● The ISOL-8 pump hydraulic circuit that will pump intervention fluid (called the BOP intervention circuit) must be able to produce sufficient pressure and flow for the intervention. If the BOP intervention requires more fluid than is available in the ISOL-8 pump circuit, you can open the circuit to seawater, providing an unlimited fluid supply (see Using Seawater as Pumped Fluid (Seawater Intake)).
