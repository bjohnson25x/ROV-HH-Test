Schilling 3-Station Valve Pack

A Schilling 3-ST valve pack typically contains:

● Two proportional valves, each on its own hydraulic circuit. You can adjust flow direction (port A or B), flow volume, and pressure.
● One high-flow valve. You can adjust flow volume but not direction. Available pressure is based on the output of the hydraulic circuit that the valve pack is attached to.

The Schilling 3-ST valve pack is typically used with an auxiliary (tooling) hydraulic circuit, but can be used with other hydraulic circuits. The screen appearance varies depending on the capabilities of the hydraulic circuit.

You can operate a valve from the screen or with a switch on the tool control pod (see Assigning Valve Operation to the Tool Pod), but you cannot use the switch and the screen control at the same time.

Note:  You can also measure pressure at the valve pack with a user-supplied gauge connected to the valve pack’s AUX PRESSURE SENSOR port.

Long press a valve button to access related functions, which may include:

● Editing flow tactile assignment by assigning flow control to a switch on the tool control pod.
● For valves with pressure control, editing pressure tactile assignments by assigning pressure control to a switch on the tool control pod.
● Editing the valve label.
● Editing the action labels for port A and port B.
● Setting the gain (the flow produced when you operate the valve using a switch on the tool control pod); see Setting Flow Rate for Proportional Valves.
