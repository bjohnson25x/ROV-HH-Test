Deleting Video Files

See Also for links to information about the Video Computer App. The Video Computer App is used only with HDEVS.

This information applies only to ROV systems that have HDEVS rather than TAVS.

You can delete multiple video segments at once from a list of videos.

1. Display the PLAYBACK screen for the Video Computer Application.
2. Select the desired project.
3. Click the Videos tab.
4. Click Delete Videos.
5. For each video segment that you want to delete, put a check mark in the box in the Delete column.

6. Click Delete.
