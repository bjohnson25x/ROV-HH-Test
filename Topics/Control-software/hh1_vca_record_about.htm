About Recording Video with the Video Computer

See Also for links to information about the Video Computer App. The Video Computer App is used only with HDEVS.

See Also for links to more information about recording on the video computer. This information applies only to ROV systems with HDEVS.

This information applies only to ROV systems that have HDEVS rather than TAVS.

Purpose: The video preview panel of the Video Computer App lets you:

● Start recording for an ROV system video stream by selecting it in the preview panel. Deselecting a video stream stops recording for that stream.

● Add and remove overlays for the selected video streams.

● Preview selected video streams with their overlays.

Access: Displays at the top of all Video Computer App screens except the PLAYBACK screen.

You can perform two types of recording on the video computer:

● In continuous backup recording, video streams are recorded on both video computer internal drives (hard drives). Simultaneous recording on both drives offers protection if one of the drives fails. When the internal drives become full, the oldest video segments are deleted to make room for new segments. You start continuous backup recording for a video stream simply by routing the stream to the video computer.
● Client recording is performed on external memory devices. In client recording, you manually start and stop recording of video segments.

The preview panel shows which video streams are being recorded, both for continuous backup recording and client recording. The number and type of video streams in the preview panel depends on how you have configured Video Type on the VIDEO CONFIGURATION screen (see Configuring Recording Details and Viewing Overlays). You use drop-down lists under the preview panel to select v ideo streams for recording.

Note:  You can also route video streams to the video computer from the ROV control system FLIGHT screen VIDEO panel (see Flight Screen Video Panel).

If a space on the video preview panel is not currently assigned to a video stream, that space displays a TITAN 4 manipulator gripper as a placeholder.

When you start up the Video Computer App, the video preview panel displays the same video streams that were being recorded when the video computer was shut down.

In the example above, the video computer has been configured to record two HD streams. In the drop-down list for selecting an HD a video stream, only HD videos are available. In a drop-down list for selecting an SD video stream, only SD videos are available.
