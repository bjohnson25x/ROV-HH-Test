Starting and Stopping Client Recording

See Also for links to more information about client recording on the video computer. This information applies only to ROV systems with HDEVS.

This information applies only to ROV systems that have HDEVS rather than TAVS.

You can start and stop client recording from:

● The preview panel of the VIDEO DASHBOARD (described in the rest of this section).
● The VIDEO panel on the FLIGHT screen of the ROV control system (described in Flight Screen Video Panel).

The preview panel of the VIDEO DASHBOARD shows all video streams currently being recorded. The progress bar for recording on the internal drives is orange.

To start client recording:

1. Ensure that you have enabled recording for the desired external memory devices (see Configuring for Client Recording).
2. Display the VIDEO DASHBOARD of the Video Computer App.
3. In the progress bar of the video stream for which you want to start video recording, click “Click to start recording.” The progress bar then turns red, and client recording begins.

Unless you stop client recording, it continues through the end of the 15-minute video segment. When that segment ends, a new client recording video segment begins.

To stop client recording click “Click to stop recording” on the red progress bar. The following things then happen:

● The progress bar turns orange.
● The client recording video segment ends, and no new one begins.

Even though you have stopped client recording, you cannot yet remove the external memory device. (You must finalize the recording session to remove the device, as described in Finalizing Client Recording for Export). Until you finalize the recording session for the external memory device, you can continue to create client recording video segments the device.
