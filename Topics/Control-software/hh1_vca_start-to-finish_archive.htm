Archiving and Deleting Video Segments

See Also for links to more information in the video recording start-to-finish guide. This guide applies only to ROV systems with HDEVS.

This information applies only to ROV systems that have HDEVS rather than TAVS.

Whenever you route video from ROV system cameras to the Video Computer App, that video is recorded on both video computer internal drives. The internal drives work in a continuous backup mode; once the drives are full, the oldest video is deleted to make room for the newest video. If you want to keep video segments that are stored on the internal drives, you must export those files before they are deleted.

You can also manually delete video segments from the internal drives to make navigation easier in the PLAYBACK screen of the Video Computer App and to make room for new video recordings.

Exporting Video

To export video from internal drives to an external memory device:

1. Attach an external memory device to the video computer. (For exporting video segments from internal drives, the external memory device receiving the files does not have to meet a speed requirement.)
2. On the PLAYBACK screen of the Video Computer App, click Export.
3. Put check marks in boxes next to all video segments that you want to export.
4. Select the destination drive from the drop-down list.
5. Click Export again.

All selected video segments are then copied to the external memory device. At the same time, recorded audio annotations are incorporated into the video segments and a video log is created.

Deleting Video from Internal Drives

Once you have archived video segments from an internal drive onto an external memory device, you can manually delete those segments from the internal drives. This will make navigation easier in the PLAYBACK screen of the Video Computer App.

To delete the video segments:

1. Display the PLAYBACK screen of the Video Computer App and click Delete Videos.
2. Put a check mark in the box next to all videos to be deleted. (To delete all the videos, put a check mark in the Delete box at the top of the column.)
3. Click Delete.

The video segments are deleted from both internal drives.

If no video remains from the time of an event, all event place markers and audio annotations for that event are also deleted.

Exporting a Still Photo of an Event

If an event place marker exists for the event, display the PLAYBACK screen of the Video Computer App and click the Event tab. Click the desired event, right click on the still photo of the event, and click Export.

If an event place marker does not exist at the event location, play back the video segment. At the desired location in the video segment, click the camera icon to insert an unplanned event. Right click on the still photo of the event and then click Export.
